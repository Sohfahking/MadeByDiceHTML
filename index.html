About The Creator

Dice the Designer is well known for her creative yet classy designs, neat workmanship, and interactive ordering experience. She began crocheting during the pandemic, after the passing of her grandmother who introduced her to the craft. Crocheting now serves as the path to her fashion designing dreams, as well as a connection to her ancestors. Thank you for considering her work and recognizing the value of art.
